Graphic design focuses on communicating a message visually. Graphic design is all around you, it's all about expressing an idea in the best way using visual story telling. Great graphic design allows you to make a positive first impression on those who looks at it. The key to mastering layout in a composition is to think like a desinger.

Photography Compositions

Photography Composition is about arranging, creating, seeing, framing and cropping. Composition must guide the viewer, their eye will make its way through the frame of a photograph and an artist tells a story within the confines of a single frame. There are few things we can compose photos using smartphones: natural light, contrast, space, color, frames and lines.
